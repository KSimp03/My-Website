Try these tasty treats inspired by your favorite Strawberryland friends!

🍓 Strawberry Shortcake’s Classic Shortcake

Ingredients: Fresh strawberries, whipped cream, sweet biscuits

Instructions: Layer sliced strawberries and whipped cream between two fluffy biscuits. Top with a whole berry and a mint leaf!

🍬 Peppermint Fizz’s Cool Peppermint Bark

Ingredients: White chocolate, crushed peppermint candies, peppermint extract

Instructions: Melt chocolate, mix in peppermint extract, spread thin, sprinkle candy on top, then refrigerate until firm. Break into pieces and serve!

🐻 Apple Dumpling’s Cinnamon Apple Bites

Ingredients: Diced apples, cinnamon, honey, puff pastry

Instructions: Wrap apple mix in small pastry squares, bake until golden brown, and drizzle with honey.

🌾 Annie Oatmeal’s Energy Trail Mix

Ingredients: Oats, raisins, almonds, dried strawberries, chocolate chips

Instructions: Mix everything in a bowl and pack it for your next outdoor adventure!
